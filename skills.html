A skill that I have is being able to play the saxophone.
The typical saxophone has around 23 keys, which you press
down make different sounds.

The alto and baritone saxophones
are pitched in the key of E♭, while the soprano and tenor
saxophones are pitched in B♭. The sax is one of the easier
instruments to learn to play.
